- Free the Captive
- Punish the Oppressors
- Protect our Wealth


Methods:

- Gambling Dens: entice foolish mortals with games of chance to drain their funds
- Loan Services: operate as a bank with insane interest rates
- Acquisition Specialists: get/make magic items, utilize wish magic when possible
