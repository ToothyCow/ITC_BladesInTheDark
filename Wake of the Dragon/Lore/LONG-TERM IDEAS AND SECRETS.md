There is a secret dragon cult, and the Tyrannus is involved

- Is he the leader, or just a disciple?

“Falafel” is actually the descendant of the monarchy, and is trying to reestablish her rule

Samantha Murph is an estranged child of the Murphisonians, an old-money sorcerer family from the Gnomish enclaves.

  

Themes

- Legacy/Inheritance
- Power/Ambition

Legacy/Inheritance:

- - : Samantha was rejected for tarnishing the family legacy
- - : Jared rejected his family legacy of naked ambition & greed
- + : Falafel is seeking to reestablish her family legacy
- +/- : Tao is wrestling to understand the legacy her father left her
- +/- : Jei-???

Power/Ambition:

- Jei is struggling to wrest control back from her patron
- Samantha is seeking to prove she is capable without the need for magic
- Jared feels powerless to fight his family
- Falafel is trying to raise support to reclaim the throne.
- Tao has had all this power thrust on her, & doesn't know what it means

  

Factions:

- Dragons (secret power)
- Ravens (reject legacy, seize power)
- Fey Courts (honor legacy, maintain power)
- Djinn Collective (redefine legacy, siphon power)

  

Dragons:

Power: "Power is the fruit of will. Those with the will to grow strong, dominate."

Inheritance: "None will wrest control from my claws. I am a dynasty unto myself."

  

Ravens Guild:

Legacy: "The old institutions are as brittle as old bones. They will snap under pressure."

Fey Courts:
