The immortal emperor is the mage that broke the world a thousand years ago. 
 
He broke the world trying to bring back his deceased wife 
 
The dimmer sisters are fragments of his wife, combined with alternate reality versions of her, his daughter, his mother. So many stories, all with a grieving, powerful mage trying to take back what he lost. 
 
 The mother of the dimmer sisters is the ghost of the immortal emperor's deceased wife, the first ghost. 
 
 This ghost is not feral, but she refuses to possess anyone or drink life. She wants to die, but more than that she wants to stop the emperor from causing further harm. However, her mind is shattered, being forced to deal with all the realities and alternate versions of herself that the emperor's shattering of the world caused, and she no longer values human life, because there's more than one of everything. 
Stepping into house means encountering fragmented reality. You are confronted with alternate realities, visions of what you could have been or are somewhere else, and time tends to cut abruptly from moment to moment. This is the mind cutting out memories to preserve your sanity.
