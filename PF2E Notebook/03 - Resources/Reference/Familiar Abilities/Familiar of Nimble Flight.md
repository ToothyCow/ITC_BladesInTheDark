# Familiar of Nimble Flight

### 

**Category** familiar; 




Even if your familiar doesn't have wings, it can sometimes move as if carried aloft by the wind. When you Cast or Sustain a hex, your familiar can Fly up to 15 feet; this movement doesn't trigger reactions.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
