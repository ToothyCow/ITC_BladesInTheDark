# Legendary Laugh
## Feat 17
### [[gnoll]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Your laugher echoes in the minds of your enemies. You can [[Actions/Demoralize|Demoralize]] creatures up to 60 feet away. Additionally, whenever you successfully Demoralize a creature, it takes 3d8 mental damage (or 6d8 mental damage on a critical success).

*Source: Pathfinder Player Core 2*
