# Bonded Focus
## Feat 14
### [[wizard]]

**Category** class; 



**Prerequisites** arcane bond
* * *
Your connection to your bonded item increases your focus pool. Whenever you Refocus while your bonded item is in your possession, you completely refill your focus pool.
