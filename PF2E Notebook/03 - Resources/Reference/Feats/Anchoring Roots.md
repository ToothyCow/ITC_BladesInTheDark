# Anchoring Roots
## Feat 5
### [[leshy]]

**Category** ancestry; 




Small roots sprout from your feet, steadying you as you move. You gain the [[Feats/Steady Balance|Steady Balance]] skill feat, even if you aren't trained in Acrobatics, and you can use the [[Actions/Anchor|Anchor]] action.
