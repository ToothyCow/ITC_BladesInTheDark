# Twirl Through
## Feat 4
### [[archetype]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** Fan Dancer Dedication
* * *
You sweep across the battlefield in a fluttering of movement honed from years of coordinating perfectly spaced movements alongside fellow dancers. When you attempt to [[Actions/Tumble Through|Tumble Through]] an enemy's space, you can use [[/act tumble-through skill=performance]]{Performance} instead of Acrobatics.
