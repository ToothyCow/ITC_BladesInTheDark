# Lasting Coercion
## Feat 2
### [[general]][[skill]]

**Category** skill; 



**Prerequisites** expert in Intimidation
* * *
When you successfully [[Actions/Coerce|Coerce]] someone, the maximum time they comply increases to a week, still determined by the GM. If you're legendary, the maximum increases to a month.
