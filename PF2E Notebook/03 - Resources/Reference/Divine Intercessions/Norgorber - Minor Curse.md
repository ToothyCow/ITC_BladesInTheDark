# Norgorber - Minor Curse

### 

**Category** curse; 




Norgorber allows thieves to get the better of you.

Whenever you [[Actions/Earn Income|Earn Income]], you earn money as if the task were 2 levels lower due to theft, loss, or bad fortune.
