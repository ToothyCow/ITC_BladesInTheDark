# Knockdown `pf2:1`

### 

**Category** offensive; 




**Requirements** The monster's last action was a successful Strike that lists Knockdown in its damage entry

* * *

**Effect** The monster attempts to [[/act trip]] the creature. This attempt neither applies nor counts toward the monster's multiple attack penalty.
