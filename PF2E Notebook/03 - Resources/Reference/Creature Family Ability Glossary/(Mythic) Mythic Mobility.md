# (Mythic) Mythic Mobility

### 

**Category** offensive; 




A mythic striker benefits from high mobility and is difficult to pin down. It always uses the extreme skill value for a creature of its level as its Acrobatics modifier.

*Source: Pathfinder War of Immortals*
