# (Mythic) Mythic Skill `pf2:0`

### 

**Category** offensive; 




**Cost** 1 Mythic Point

* * *

**Effect** The creature attempts its next skill check with a +4 bonus and is considered to have mythic proficiency for that check. This applies to one skill when this ability is gained and a second skill if the creature is 12th level or higher.

*Source: Pathfinder War of Immortals*
