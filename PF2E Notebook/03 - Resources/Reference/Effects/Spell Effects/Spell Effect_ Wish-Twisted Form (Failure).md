# Spell Effect: Wish-Twisted Form (Failure)
## Level 5
### 






Granted by [[Spells/Wish-Twisted Form|Wish-Twisted Form]].

The target gains weakness to the chosen damage type, it takes a -10-foot status penalty to all its Speeds, and a -1 status penalty to its saving throws. On a critical failure, the target also takes a -1 status penalty to AC.
