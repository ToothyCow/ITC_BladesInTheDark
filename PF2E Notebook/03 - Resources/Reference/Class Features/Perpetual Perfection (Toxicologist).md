# Perpetual Perfection (Toxicologist)
## Feat 17
### [[alchemist]]

**Category** classfeature; 




You have perfected your perpetual infusions, allowing you to use [[Class Features/Quick Alchemy|Quick Alchemy]] to create even more powerful items at no cost. The items you can select depend on your research field and must be in your formula book.

Choose two common 11th-level or lower alchemical poisons.
