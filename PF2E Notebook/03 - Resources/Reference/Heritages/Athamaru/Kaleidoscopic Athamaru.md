# Kaleidoscopic Athamaru

### 






Your scales are a spectrum of color that shimmers in the light. In areas of bright light or dim light, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Performance checks. You also gain the [[Actions/Dazzle Seeker|Dazzle Seeker]] reaction.

*Source: Pathfinder Howl of the Wild*
