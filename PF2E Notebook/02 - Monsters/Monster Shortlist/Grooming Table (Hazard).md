**Hardness** 5, **HP** 15
**Grabber Arms** [reaction] ([attack](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=15)); **Trigger** A creature steps adjacent to the table. **Effect** The arms make a melee Strike against the triggering creature.  
**Melee** Massage +13 ([deadly d10](https://2e.aonprd.com/Traits.aspx?ID=174)), **Damage** 2d6 bludgeoning and the target is grabbed and pulled onto the table (Escape DC 16). The target takes 1d6 bludgeoning damage at the end of each of its turns as long as it’s caught and must succeed at a DC 12 Fortitude save or gain the [[Fatigued]] condition.
