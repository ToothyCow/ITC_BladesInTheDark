# Story
When the PCs approach the door to this chamber, they can hear an angry bellowing from within. A player who succeeds at a DC 12 Perception check recognizes [[Quonx]]'s shouting.
> The tiled walls of this room are colored a soothing sea-foam green. Three cushioned tables occupy the center of the room,each with a plethora of mechanical arms ending in various nozzles, manipulator digits, and other implements. A series of chairs runs along the starboard side of the room, with apertures in the wall behind them. The air is humid and smells of sandalwood.


This area is an automated spa for the Emerald Empyrean’s passengers. A trio of massage tables could relax three guests, while gentle music and a moisturizing steam were pumped in through vents near the ceiling. Guests seeking exfoliating scrubs or hydrating seaweed masks could sit in one of the chairs, and robotic arms would provide the service they requested. Unfortunately, M2’s ideas of relaxation have made this area unsafe.

[[Quonx]] is currently trapped on the central grooming table, being kneaded and pummeled to a ridiculous degree. He stumbled into this room after bolting through the observation deck and was immediately grabbed by the table because M2 deemed him too tense. His constant struggling hasn’t lessened the AI’s opinions of the skittermander's state of mind. 

The PCs can release Quonx by spending a full action and succeeding at a DC 16 Engineering check while adjacent to the table.
